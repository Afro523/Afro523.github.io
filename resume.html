Summary

Hi my name is Josh and I am a graduate student currently working on my masters in the Seidenberg School for Computer Science at Pace University. I have always been facinated by building things from scratch, and I have been doing just that with my computers since I was 16. It was the love for building stuff that made me choose environmental sciences and mathematics as my undergrad, to see how our city, state, country, world, galaxy, and the entire universe is connected.

It was in my last year of my undergrad degree that I realized that I did not just want to eat sleep and breath only 1 subject for the rest of my life, and that is what a doctorate in Astro-Geology (my desired degree) would have led to. Instead I chose to go the path where the knowledge base is always growing and it can then be applied to a million different scenarios, with each one being more unique then the last.
